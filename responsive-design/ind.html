About Hyundai Motors India

Hyundai Motor India Ltd. (HMIL) is a subsidiary of Hyundai Motor Company, South Korea. Established in 1996, it is the second-largest car manufacturer in India and a leading exporter of passenger vehicles. The company operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Sriperumbudur, Tamil Nadu, with a production capacity of over 850,000 units annually. Hyundai India is known for popular models like the Creta, Venue, i20, and Verna, and has been expanding its electric vehicle portfolio with models like the Ioniq 5.
